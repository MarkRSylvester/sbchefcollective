Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book?

Please navigate to our Contact Page and submit your inquiry. Once we receive your request, we will setup an initial phone call with you and see how we can best fulfill your needs! We will then set you up with one of our amazing chefs, depending on the specific style and nature of your event.

How does the payment process work?

With our Chef Collective, we will write you up a menu proposal with a set rate for your event, depending on your group size and meal specifications. We require you to pay half of the total fee in order to secure your date and the second half on the day of your event.

Is there a cancellation period?

Yes! You have up to 2 weeks before your event to receive a full refund. Anything after 2 weeks will be dealt with on a case by case basis, understanding that we are living in complicated times with unforeseen plan changes. We get it!

What does it cost?

Depending on the scale, size and nature of your event, the cost typically ranges from $125-$175 per person or an hourly rate ($65-$140) + groceries for meal delivery services. Please inquire for a quote catered directly to your needs.

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of culinary services including:

  • Private Chef Services for intimate dinners and events
  • Event Catering for larger gatherings
  • Cooking Classes and demonstrations
  • Weekly Meal Preparation
  • Wine Pairing consultation
  • Kitchen Organization services
  • Full-Time Estate Chef Placement

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! Our chefs are experienced in preparing meals for various dietary needs including:

  • Vegetarian and Vegan
  • Gluten-Free
  • Dairy-Free
  • Keto and Paleo
  • Food Allergies and Sensitivities

Please let us know your dietary requirements when booking, and we'll ensure they are accommodated.

What is your service area?

We primarily serve the Santa Barbara area, including Montecito, Carpinteria, Goleta, and surrounding communities. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our primary service area. For estate chef placement, we work with clients nationwide and internationally.

What is included in your service?

Our standard service includes:

  • Menu planning and customization
  • Grocery shopping and ingredient sourcing
  • On-site preparation and cooking
  • Professional service and presentation
  • Kitchen cleanup after service
  • All necessary cooking equipment

Additional services such as rentals, staff, and bar service can be arranged upon request.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least:

  • 2-3 weeks in advance for private dining
  • 4-6 weeks for special events and holidays
  • 2-3 months for large events and weddings

However, we understand that last-minute requests happen and will do our best to accommodate when possible.

Can you handle large events?

Yes! We have experience catering events of all sizes, from intimate dinners to large gatherings of 200+ guests. For larger events, we provide additional staff and equipment as needed to ensure smooth service.
